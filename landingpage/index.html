Every visit to a park can feel like a missed opportunity when you spot a bird or flower but can't remember its name or where and when you saw it. Our app turns that frustration into discovery by giving you instant, park-specific field guides and a simple camera interface that logs every sighting with the exact time and location. No more fumbling through bulky books or fragmented apps: you get a concise species catalog for your chosen park, AI-backed photo uploads, and a personal dashboard to track and revisit your favorite finds. It's the seamless way to deepen your nature knowledge and capture every moment of your outdoor adventures.
